Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

ConnectSphere ("we", "us", or "the app") is a social platform that lets you create a profile, share posts, images and short videos ("reels"), react and comment, follow other people, and send direct messages. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have. By creating an account or using ConnectSphere, you agree to this policy.

Information you provide

  • Account information: your name, username, email address, and password. Passwords are stored only in a securely hashed form and are never readable by us.
  • Profile details: anything you choose to add, such as a bio, location, website, avatar, and cover photo.
  • Content you create: posts, comments, images, videos/reels, reactions, and the people you follow.
  • Messages: the direct messages you send and receive, so we can deliver them to the intended recipient.

Information collected automatically

  • Usage data: basic activity needed to run the app, such as which posts you view, like, or save, and unread message counts.
  • Device & connection data: standard technical information (such as device type and network requests) used to operate and secure the service.

How we use your information

  • To create and maintain your account and profile.
  • To display your feed, explore, reels, notifications, and search results.
  • To deliver real-time chat, presence, and message notifications.
  • To keep the service secure, prevent abuse, and moderate harmful content.
  • To fix bugs and improve reliability and performance.

How your information is stored and shared

We use trusted third-party providers to run ConnectSphere. We do not sell your personal information, and we do not share it for advertising.

  • Media hosting (Cloudinary): images and videos you upload are stored and delivered through Cloudinary.
  • Database & hosting: your account data and content are stored on our secure cloud database and application hosting providers.
  • Legal reasons: we may disclose information if required by law or to protect the rights, safety, and security of our users and the service.

Public vs. private content

Posts, reels, comments, your profile, and reactions may be visible to other users. If you set your account to Private in Settings, your content visibility is limited accordingly. Direct messages are visible only to you and the recipient. Please remember that anything you make public can be seen and re-shared by others.

Data retention and deletion

We keep your information for as long as your account is active. You can delete your own posts, reels, and comments at any time from within the app. If you want your account and associated personal data removed, contact us at the email below and we will delete it, except where we are required to retain certain data for legal or security reasons.

Security

We use industry-standard measures to protect your data, including hashed passwords and secure, token-based authentication. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, but we work to protect your information and to promptly address any issues.

Children's privacy

ConnectSphere is not intended for children under 13 (or the minimum age required in your country). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under this age. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can remove it.

Your rights and choices

  • Access and update your profile information at any time in the app.
  • Control your account visibility with the Private Account setting.
  • Change your username and password in Settings.
  • Request deletion of your account and personal data by contacting us.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Significant changes may also be communicated within the app.
